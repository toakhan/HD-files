Hobbies

Sports

Playing basketball was my favorite hobby. I have many fond memories of playing with my friends after school.

I also enjoyed playing soccer with my dad on the weekends. It was a great way for us to bond.

Reading

I loved reading adventure books when I was a child. I remember getting lost in the stories and feeling like I was part of the action.

I also enjoyed reading about animals and learning about different species. It was fascinating to me.

Toys

Action Figures

I had a collection of action figures that I would play with for hours. I remember making up elaborate stories for them and acting them out.

One of my favorites was a Batman action figure that my uncle gave me. It was so cool!

Legos

I loved building things with Legos when I was a child. It was so satisfying to see something take shape from nothing.

I remember spending hours building a giant castle and then playing with my action figures inside it.
