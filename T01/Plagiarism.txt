Compulsory task 3: Plagiarism


1. Plagiarism means when someone takes work or ideas of others and claims that the copied work or idea

is their own. If such work needs to be incorporatrd, a full acknowledgement is required.


2. Four different types of plagiarism are: Direct plagiarism, self plagiarism, mosaic plagiarism 

and accidental plagiarism.


3. Avoiding plagiarism: Keeping track of the sources consulted and crediting the original author in 

the reference list.
